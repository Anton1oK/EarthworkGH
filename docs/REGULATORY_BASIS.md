# Regulatory Basis

Checked on 2026-06-01. Standards must be reviewed again before issuing working
documentation because amendments and project-specific requirements can change.

## Drawing Outputs

- `GOST 21.508-2020`, especially sections 7 and 8, governs organization-of-relief
  and earth-mass plans. Section 8 calls for the square method as the usual
  calculation method, actual/project/working marks at square corners, zero-work
  lines, 45-degree hatch for excavation areas, volumes per square or other
  figure, and cut/fill totals by grid column. A 20 m square is the default;
  10, 25, 40 and 50 m sides are also allowed depending on relief and required
  accuracy.
- `GOST R 21.101-2020` supplies general SPDS requirements for design and working
  documentation.

## Earthwork Engineering

- `SP 45.13330.2017` with amendments 1, 2 and 3 governs execution and acceptance
  of earthworks, bases and foundations. It covers surface-water handling,
  excavations, fill, backfill and compaction. Temporary excavation slopes cannot
  be selected from geometry alone.
- `SP 22.13330.2016` with amendments 1-5 governs soil bases of buildings and
  structures, including low-rise buildings.
- `SP 126.13330.2017` with amendments 1 and 2 governs geodetic work and setting
  out in construction.
- `SP 82.13330.2016` with amendments 1, 2 and 3 is relevant to territory
  improvement and final landscaping.

## Important Boundary

The plugin may generate drafting geometry and calculation aids that follow the
graphic conventions above. It must not auto-certify excavation slopes,
groundwater measures, bearing conditions, compaction requirements or final
construction safety without project-specific engineering inputs and review.
